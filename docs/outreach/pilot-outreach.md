# GRID Pilot Outreach Scripts
_Last updated: 2026-04-22 · Owner: Raj_

Target: 10 event companies in Mumbai / Delhi NCR / Bengaluru / Goa.
Pitch: free 90-day pilot of the Event Company OS. Landing page: https://grid.events/pilot
Application form writes to `instabook_interests` with `role='event_company'`, `role_specific_data.pilot=true`.

Sequence: cold WhatsApp → follow-up WA → email (if no WA response in 48h) → LinkedIn DM (C-level) → Raj voice note.

---

## 1. Cold WhatsApp — first touch

**When:** weekday, 10:30–12:00 or 16:00–18:00 IST. Not Sunday.

```
Hi [First Name] — Raj here, founder of GRID (grid.events).

Your team runs [rough # / type] events a month. The operational lift —
vendor call sheets, day-of coordination, BOQs, payouts across 10+ vendors
per event — that's exactly what GRID is built for.

We're taking 10 Indian event companies into a free 90-day pilot.
You'd get white-glove onboarding (we set up your first 3 events with you),
founder hotline, and grandfathered pricing post-pilot.

30-min walkthrough this week?

https://grid.events/pilot
```

**Dos:** use first name. Reference their actual event mix if you know it (look at their IG/site before sending). Drop the link.
**Don'ts:** don't send "Hey hope you're doing well" filler. Don't pitch "AI-powered" or "revolutionary." Direct only.

---

## 2. WhatsApp follow-up — 48h after first touch, no reply

```
[First Name] — quick nudge. We have 3 slots filled, 7 open.
Mumbai pilot starts May 5. If you want in, 30 mins this week is all I need.

Or reply NO and I'll stop following up — no hard feelings.
```

**Why it works:** scarcity + explicit opt-out. The opt-out is real — honor it.

---

**Subject:** `Free 90-day pilot — Event Company OS (India, 10 spots)`

```
  — You run 3 real events through GRID in the first 30 days
  — 30-min weekly feedback call
  — Permission to quote you publicly if you love it

Apply in 90 seconds: https://grid.events/pilot
Or reply to this and I'll call you.

Raj
+91 96-XXXXXXXX · rajesh@snap-r.com
```

## 4. LinkedIn DM — founder / CEO target, no reply anywhere

```
[First Name] — we met in spirit through [mutual / event reference].

I'm building GRID — the Event Company OS. Free 90-day pilot for 10 Indian
event companies. I want [Company] on the list.

If it's a no, say so. If not, 30 mins this week?
https://grid.events/pilot
```

**Keep it shorter than the email.** LinkedIn readers skim.

## 5. Raj voice note (WA) — for warm leads after a 1st call

_30 seconds max. Speak in the same language the call was in (English / Hindi)._

> "Hey [First Name], Raj here. Followed up after our chat.
> I spoke to the team, pushed your company to the top of the pilot list.
> One thing I forgot to mention — we'll build the [specific workflow they asked about]
> before your first event. You good to go for [date]?
> If yes, reply GO and I'll send the onboarding kit. If not, no stress."

## Qualification checklist (before investing more than 1 WA message)

Disqualify fast. Spend time on the right 20.

- [ ] Runs ≥ 4 events / month
- [ ] Based in Mumbai / Delhi NCR / Bengaluru / Goa (Sprint A coverage)
- [ ] Works with ≥ 3 vendor categories per event (artist + AV + photo min.)
- [ ] Has a team (not solo planner — solo doesn't feel the pain enough)
- [ ] Some digital maturity (uses WA Business, has IG, runs Google Sheets for ops)

## Follow-up cadence (don't overdo)

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| 0   | WA      | Cold message (#1) |
| 2   | WA      | Nudge (#2) |
| 5   | Email   | Long form (#3) |
| 9   | LinkedIn | DM founder (#4) |
| 14  | —       | Stop. Move on. |

**Never** send > 4 touches across all channels before stopping. Respect is part of the brand.

---

## After they apply

1. Confirm receipt within 2 hours (WA: "Got it. Walkthrough call Thursday 4pm?")
2. 30-min walkthrough: screen-share a real demo event file (Malabar Hill Wedding seeded in staging)
3. If fit: onboarding kit via email (API keys, first-event checklist, WA group invite)
4. First event set up together within 7 days

---

## Messaging cheatsheet

**Words to use:** operating system, event file, call sheet, vendor, BOQ, pilot,
"we set it up with you", free, India, founder.

**Words to avoid:** AI-powered, revolutionary, disrupt, solution, platform,
synergy, game-changer, onboard you (passive), democratize.

**Tone:** direct, Indian-market, slightly founder-scrappy. No corporate gloss.
Raj-voice: short sentences, no em-dash overuse, zero filler.
