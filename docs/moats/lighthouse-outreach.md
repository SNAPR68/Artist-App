- Known founder who's vocal on LinkedIn or in industry press (lighthouse = visible).
- Pain points already aired publicly (lost deposits, disputes, ghosting).

## Pilot offer (same for all 10)

- ₹0 for first 30 days. ₹15K/mo starting day 31. Month-to-month, cancel anytime.
- White-glove migration — we import your existing bookings from CSV/email.
- One dedicated engineer (me, for now) in your team's Slack/WhatsApp for 30 days.
- Feature request promise: anything you flag gets scoped within 48h, shipped within 7 days if it's a ≤1-week build.
- Data exit clause: full export at any time, signed NDA on both sides.

## What success looks like

- 3 of 10 respond within 2 weeks.
- 2 of 3 sign the pilot.
- 1 of 2 becomes a reference customer willing to be named in marketing.
- Reference customer drives 3-5 warm intros for the next wave.

## What to NOT do

- No cold demo links. Nobody will click.
- No "book a 30-minute call" CTA. Wastes their time and yours.
- No "we're building the future of XYZ" language. They've seen 40 of those.
- No name-dropping investors. These founders don't care.
- No discount negotiation. Price is price. ₹15K is already the lighthouse rate.
